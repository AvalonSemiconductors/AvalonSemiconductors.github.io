Mixer ConfigurationÔÉÅ

The mixer is responsible for combining the individual samples of all 8 SIDs, into a single sample to be sent to the outputs. Currently, this mixer simply adds all the samples together, however, this would ordinarily result in the addition overflowing the 16-bit sample value.

Therefor, the mixed sample needs to be right-shifted to fit into the 16-bit limit. However, this comes at the expense of audio quality if only a single, or a few SIDs are used. In an effort to get the best audio quality using any given amount of SIDs, the MMODE setting in the Global Configuration Register decides by how much to right-shift the sample.
